PRODUCT INFORMATION:

Automotive Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a physical protective film, covering the surface of your car’s paint to avoid external damage. Proteq’s 3 Series Paint Protection Film is colourless, transparent and is amazingly flexible. It doesn’t affect the appearance of your car. In fact, it just makes it look better!
